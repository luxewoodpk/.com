❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver across Pakistan? ▼
Yes! We deliver nationwide across Pakistan. Cash on Delivery (COD) is available everywhere.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Delivery typically takes 3-7 working days depending on your city. Major cities like Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad usually receive within 3-4 days.
What type of wood do you use? ▼
We use premium quality solid wood including Sheesham (Rosewood), Kikar, and Teak. All our wood is carefully selected for durability and beauty.
Do you accept custom orders? ▼
Yes! We love custom orders. Contact us on WhatsApp with your requirements — size, design, wood type — and we will give you a quote within 24 hours.
Can I return or exchange a product? ▼
If the product arrives damaged or is different from what you ordered, we will replace it. Contact us within 48 hours of receiving the product with photos.
How do I place an order? ▼
Simply add products to your cart and checkout, OR contact us directly on WhatsApp. We will confirm your order and arrange delivery.
Are your products handcrafted? ▼
Yes! Every single piece is crafted by hand by our skilled artisans in Silanwali. No mass production — each item is unique.
